🗺️ Delivery Phases
  • 1
    Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4) — Provider & Enrollee portals. OTP generation + validation. Member eligibility check. WhatsApp confirmation webhook. Claim auto-creation.
  • 2
    Phase 2 (Weeks 5–7) — Advocate portal. Member 360° view. FWA dashboard. Audit trail. Advocate OTP with logging. Gym email trigger.
  • 3
    Phase 3 (Weeks 8–9) — Payment & top-up flow. Paystack integration. Discount engine. Claim batch submission. Pay advice PDF generation.
  • 4
    Phase 4 (Week 10) — FWA → claim reversal API pipeline. Utilization reporting module. UAT, load testing, security audit.
  • 5
    Phase 5 (Week 11–12) — Pilot with 2 gyms + 200 members. Hypercare period. Go-live rollout plan.
